About Liu Zhengyao Lawyer

China-based lawyer focused on criminal defense, Web3 and cross-border legal matters.

Liu Zhengyao is a partner at Zhongwen Law Firm Shanghai Office. His practice focuses on criminal defense, Web3 and virtual asset-related legal matters, online financial risk cases, and China-related cross-border legal services.

He works with individuals, families, companies and Web3 project teams to understand the facts, organize evidence, identify legal risks, and build practical strategies under Chinese law.

Professional Focus

His work covers criminal defense procedures, crypto-related investigations, USDT and OTC transaction risks, frozen bank accounts, online fraud-related matters, cybercrime allegations, and cross-border legal communication.

Working Approach

Liu emphasizes clear communication, evidence-based analysis, procedural awareness and realistic risk assessment. The goal is to help clients understand what has happened, what may happen next, and what can be done within the legal process.
